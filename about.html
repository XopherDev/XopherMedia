About Me

Greetings, I'm Chris, a web development aficionado proudly based in the vibrant state of Arizona. With a profound passion for crafting digital experiences, I've devoted over 15 years to perfecting my craft in this dynamic field. Allow me to share my story and why I'm convinced that I'm the ideal candidate for your web development needs.

Throughout my journey, I've garnered a wealth of experience that sets me apart. One of the defining moments in my career was achieving the esteemed position of senior developer at Stericycle, Inc. There, I embraced the company's mission and contributed wholeheartedly to its success. My unwavering dedication to your organization's goals is non-negotiable.

My unique value lies in my ability to bridge the gap between business objectives and technological solutions. I understand that effective web development is not just about code but also about aligning every effort with your overarching vision. My approach revolves around delivering swift results without compromising on long-term value; it's about achieving efficiency without sacrificing quality.

When it comes to the technical toolbox, I come prepared. My skillset encompasses a range of indispensable tools, from Adobe ColdFusion and Lucee to WordPress, APIs, accessibility, and SEO. Whether it's building a custom web application or optimizing your online presence, I have the tools and expertise to make it happen.

My journey in web development began in college, where I cultivated my initial fascination with the web. While largely self-taught, my relentless passion drove me to gain practical experience. I embarked on my professional path as a web designer at two small startups, and it was during this time that I stumbled upon the captivating world of databases. This revelation transformed me into a full-fledged web developer, and I've had the privilege of working for both small businesses and large corporations. Loyalty has always been a core value for me, and I remained steadfast with each employer until external circumstances led to office closures.

My commitment to growth and excellence has led me to my current position. I took a deliberate break to focus on expanding my horizons. As a perpetual learner, I'm always exploring new architectural paradigms and frameworks. One area of particular intrigue is the realm of artificial intelligence, where I'm eagerly immersing myself in the latest developments and discoveries.

Looking ahead, my intent is crystal clear: I aspire to leverage my extensive skills and experiences to facilitate the prosperity of companies like yours. Your success is not just a goal but a driving force behind everything I do.

In summation, I'm Chris Tessay, an Arizona web development virtuoso with a rich tapestry of experience, an insatiable appetite for knowledge, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Let's collaborate to turn your web development aspirations into tangible achievements.
